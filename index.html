About Me

Background

For the past seven years I have been a call-taker at a 24/7 IT hotline, working for a well known healthcare company. The work mainy revolves around security and access managment, as well as direct software and hardware troubleshooting and support. The environment is fast paced, and more often than not there is a high volume of calls, but all in all I find working directly with the clients to be both fun and rewarding.

Before I worked in IT I was an aspiring musician. After high school I enrolled in community college to pursue my degree in music performance. While I never did become a professional musician, songwriting is still a passion of mine and I still continue to write music in my free time.

> Current

Even as I type this, I am currently a full-time student at Epicodus, a school for those who wish to learn computer programming. I have chosen to pursue a career code for a multitude of reasons, the bulk of which would likely bore you, but of those reasons I believe a few are worth illistrating:
